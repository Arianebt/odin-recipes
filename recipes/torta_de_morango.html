Strawberry Pie

Strawberry Pie is an easy and quick dessert to prepare, besides having a beautiful appearance on the table. It's a perfect option that goes well with family gatherings, a birthday party, or a special lunch dessert.

Ingredients

  • Dough:
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 egg yolks
    • 200 grams unsalted butter, cubed
    • 4 tablespoons sugar
    • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • Filling:
    • 350 milliliters milk
    • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
    • 1 egg yolk
    • 395 grams sweetened condensed milk
  • Topping:
    • 250 milliliters hot water
    • 25 grams strawberry-flavored gelatin powder
    • 250 milliliters cold water
    • 250 grams strawberries, sliced

Preparation

  1. Dough:
    1. In a stand mixer, add the all-purpose flour, egg yolks, unsalted butter, sugar, and baking powder. Mix until the dough is homogeneous.
    2. Distribute the dough in a springform pan and prick it with a fork to prevent inflation.
    3. Bake in a preheated oven at 180 degrees Celsius for 15 minutes.
    4. Remove from the oven and let it cool.
  2. Filling:
    1. In a large glass, add the milk, cornstarch, dissolve well, and pour into a saucepan for the filling.
    2. Add the sweetened condensed milk, egg yolk, and stir until thickened.
  3. Topping:
    1. Boil water in a saucepan and then add the strawberry-flavored gelatin.
    2. Continue stirring well to dissolve and add a little cold water.
    3. Once dissolved, set aside for 2 hours.
  4. Assembly:
    1. Place the filling over the dough, add the sliced strawberries, and cover with the strawberry gelatin.
    2. Refrigerate for 3 hours.
