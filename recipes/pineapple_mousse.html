Pineapple Mousse

Pineapple mousse, a perfect choice to end a meal on a high note or to sweeten a special moment! It's a selection that will surely please every palate.

Ingredients (serves 10)

  • Pineapple:
    • 1 peeled and cored pineapple
    • 4 cups water
    • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • Condensed milk:
    • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • Heavy cream:
    • 1 can heavy cream
  • Pineapple gelatin:
    • 2 boxes pineapple-flavored gelatin

Preparation: 30 minutes

  1. Place the four cups of water in a saucepan.
  2. Dice the pineapple into small pieces and add it to the saucepan with water.
  3. Add the two tablespoons of sugar to the water with the pineapple.
  4. Let it boil for 5 minutes.
  5. Strain the pineapple and reserve the boiled water.
  6. Add the gelatins to the water to dissolve.
  7. Pour the water with the dissolved gelatin into a blender and blend with the condensed milk and heavy cream.
  8. In a dish, place the diced pineapple and pour the blender mixture over it.
  9. Refrigerate until set.
  10. Now, it's time to enjoy because it's truly delicious!
