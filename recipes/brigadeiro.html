Brigadeiro

Traditional Brazilian Chocolate Brigadeiro Recipe, the no-bake bite-size sweets with only 4 ingredients. Call them truffles, fudge balls or just brigadeiros, they are sure to make you fall in love with them. So rich, dense and chocolatey, perfect for every celebration!

Ingredients

  • Condensed milk: Use sweetened condensed milk for best results.
  • Butter: Salted or unsalted.
  • Cocoa powder: A good quality one makes all the difference.
  • Sprinkles: Any kind, or just omit.

Steps:

  1. Add the condensed milk, butter, and sifted cocoa powder in a pan set over a low heat.
  2. Mix well with a spatula to get a smooth paste.
  3. Continue to stir until it thickens, which might take up to 15 minutes or so.
  4. You know the paste is cooked well when it coats the back of the spatula and you can see the bottom of the pan for at least 3-4 seconds when you drag the spatula through it.
  5. Remove it from the heat, and leave to cool completely.
  6. You can then shape it into balls, then coat with sprinkles or desiccated coconut.
